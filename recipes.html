Our Products

Our current crop of award winning extra virgins from Australia include a creamy, green and mild Hojiblanca; in the medium-intensity category is the herbaceous and peppery Coratina and the grassy early harvest Picual. In the robust category we are featuring a French varietal, Picholine. From Chile is a big healthfully robust Koroneiki.

Syracuse just got spicier! We are pleased to offer you a rotating selection of flavorful spicy olive oils. We've included a customer favorite, Chipotle, along with exotic olive oils like Harissa and Baklouti. Remember to check out the spicy sweet Serrano Honey Vinegar and a recent addition, Jalapeno White Balsamic.

Pineapple White Balsamic pairs well with any and all of our spicies! Don't forget to check out Tequila Jalapeno mustard and the Spicy Sampler collec- tion of sea salts, too.
